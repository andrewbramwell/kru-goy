About Us

Welcome to Kru Goy UK

Founded in Manchester's Chinatown, Kru Goy UK supports Thai women in building successful careers in the wellness industry. Our founder combines traditional Thai healing knowledge with practical British business experience to provide comprehensive training that goes beyond technique.

We're dedicated massage therapy experts who believe that every Thai woman deserves the opportunity to build a thriving, independent career in the UK's growing wellness industry.

Starting your massage therapy career shouldn't be overwhelming! Here's how we make it achievable:

  1. Determine your career goals: Contact us to discuss your aspirations, experience level, and what you hope to achieve. The clearer your vision, the better we can guide your training journey.
  2. Research our training programmes: Book a consultation to learn about our courses, meet our instructors, and see if we're the right fit. We'll explain qualifications, show our facilities, and ensure you feel confident about your choice.
  3. Choose your course: We offer flexible training options for every skill level and budget. Select from foundation courses, advanced techniques, or specialised treatments.
  4. Prepare for your training: Once you've chosen your programme and feel confident in your decision, enrol and secure your place. Your transformation begins!
  5. Complete your training: We'll support you through practical sessions, theory lessons, and help you feel prepared and confident to start your professional practice.
  6. Launch your career: After certification, you'll have the skills and business knowledge to build a successful massage therapy practice and achieve financial independence.
